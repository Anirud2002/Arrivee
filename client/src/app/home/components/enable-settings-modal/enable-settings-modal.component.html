By enabling location, the app will be able to know your exact position and send notification if you are near any location you've set!

To turn on Location:

1. Click on "Location".

2. Select "While Using the App" Or "Always".

By enabling notification, the app will be able to remind you when you are inside the radius of any location you've set!

To turn on Notification:

1. Click on "Notifications".

2. Turn on "Allow Notifications".
